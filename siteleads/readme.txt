== Installation ==

1. Go to **Plugins > Add New** in your WordPress dashboard.
2. Search for **SiteLeads**.
3. Click **Install Now**, then **Activate**.
4. The SiteLeads menu will appear in your WordPress sidebar.
5. Add your contact channels, customize the widget, and optionally set up your free AI chatbot.

A free SiteLeads account is required to use the AI chatbot and access the leads dashboard. The contact widget works without an account.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What does SiteLeads do? =
SiteLeads adds a floating contact widget to your WordPress site with all the channels your visitors already use — WhatsApp, phone, email, Messenger, Telegram, and more. It also includes a free AI chatbot that automatically engages visitors, answers their questions, and captures their contact information — 24/7, without your involvement.

= Is SiteLeads a live chat plugin? =
No. SiteLeads connects visitors to the channels you already use to close deals — WhatsApp, phone, email. The AI chatbot qualifies and captures leads automatically, without requiring you to be online.

= Is the AI chatbot really free? =
Yes. The AI chatbot is free and optional. The free plan includes 1 AI chatbot, a knowledge base built from up to 30 pages of your website, 2 uploaded training files, and up to 5 captured leads per month.

= Do I need to know how to code? =
No. SiteLeads is set up entirely from the WordPress dashboard. No code required at any step.

= Which channels are supported? =
WhatsApp, WhatsApp Business, phone (click to call), email, Facebook Messenger, Telegram, Instagram DM, SMS, TikTok, WeChat, Snapchat, Discord, Google Maps, X/Twitter, and more.

= Does the AI chatbot work with any website content? =
The AI chatbot learns from the pages you select on your website, plus any documents you upload (PDF, DOCX). In the free plan, it can read up to 30 pages. In Pro, it can crawl the full site.

= Where are my leads stored? =
Captured leads are stored in your SiteLeads account and accessible directly from your WordPress dashboard.

= Do I need a SiteLeads account? =
A free SiteLeads account is required to use the AI chatbot, manage your knowledge base, and access the leads dashboard. The basic contact widget works without an account.

= Will SiteLeads slow down my website? =
No. The widget loads asynchronously and is optimized for performance.

= Does it work with my theme and page builder? =
Yes. SiteLeads works with all standard WordPress themes and page builders including Elementor, Divi, Gutenberg, WPBakery, and more.

= What's the difference between the contact widget and the AI chatbot? =
The contact widget gives visitors instant access to your channels — WhatsApp, phone, email, Telegram, and more. The AI chatbot is configured like a channel, but has features like: starts conversations, answers questions, and captures contact details. Both work together. The AI chatbot is free and optional.

= How does the AI chatbot qualify visitors? =
The AI chatbot is trained on your website content. When a visitor starts a conversation, it answers their questions and naturally asks for their name and contact details — which are saved as leads in your WordPress dashboard. No scripts to write. No human needed.
